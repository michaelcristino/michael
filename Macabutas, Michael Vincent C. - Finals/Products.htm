BE YOUR OWN KIND OF BEAUTIFUL

Sylvester Salon is proud to offer our clients a truly unique salon experience. In addition to the services we offer, we carry a variety of the finest professional beauty products on the market. We also sell jewelry, wearables, art, products by local Cavite artists and designers, and other accessories in our boutique.




BEAUTY PRODUCTS

Sylvester Salon carries the beauty and hair products we love to use ourselves. With our wide selection our carefully curated hair (and skincare) products, there is something for virtually every hair type. In addition, our selections are made with an emphasis on quality and affordability, as well as consideration for the environment. With our styling products and healthy hair regimens, our clients can have the salon experience every day in their own home.

We also have the latest in professional blowdryers, curling irons, flat irons and brushes for every hair type.




JEWELRY, GIFTS & ACCESSORIES

Our boutique is home to a selection of beautiful jewelry, gifts, scarves, bags, and other accessories! Many of our jewelry is handmade by local Cavite artists.



LOCAL DESIGNS

We are proud to carry products by local artists and designers. Cavite is filled with such incredible talent, and Sylvester Salon is happy to support and showcase that talent in our boutique. We feature and hang a different artist’s work every 2 months on the walls of Sylvester Salon.
